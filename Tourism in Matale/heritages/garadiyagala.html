Heritage Site

Garadigala Temple

On the Adiagala road from Galewela. m. When you reach Damunumulla village after going for 3, you can go to Garadigala temple. The cave temple and the rock next to it are located on the same rock. It is said that the name Garadigala has been obtained from the fact that Katara is carved on the rock like a garadi. K. m. With a length of about 1 km, the rock is about 200 meters high. The rock plateau that appears between the forests creates a beautiful view. Several caves and sacred features like Bodhiya Dagaba can be seen on the temple grounds. Five cave caves, cave statues, and two rock inscriptions have been designated as archaeological monuments. By climbing the steps you can enter the dagaba on the rock. On it, you can see a beautiful view of the plains with lakes, forests, and paddies around Galewela.
